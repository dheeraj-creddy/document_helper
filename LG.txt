COMPLETE COMPANY HISTORY
ERA 1: FOUNDATION (1947-1959)

Koo In-hwoi establishes Lak Hui Chemical Industrial Corp
First product: "Lucky Cream" (Korea's first makeup cream)
Company name meaning: "Giving joy to all"
Lays foundation for future LG empire

1952

Lucky becomes first Korean plastics manufacturer
Demonstrates innovation leadership early

1955

Launches Lucky toothpaste
"Lucky" brand becomes household name
Expands product line

1958 - GoldStar Born

October 1: GoldStar Co., Ltd. established
Electronics division of Lucky Chemical
Post-Korean War reconstruction era
Mission: Provide domestically-produced electronics

1959

Produces Korea's FIRST:

Radio (model A-501 with GoldStar trademark)
6-transistor radio
4-tube radio
12-inch electric fan
Automatic telephone


Establishes electronics manufacturing leadership

1960s

Expands product categories

Forges relationship with Hitachi

1965

Launches Korea's first domestic refrigerator
Revolutionizes Korean households

1969

Introduces first Korean washing machines

1970

GoldStar goes public
Opens capital markets access

1974

Lak Hui Chemical renamed Lucky Co., Ltd.
Consolidates brand identity

1976
